ECE Graduate Student
Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology, Bangladesh

I am an ECE graduate Master's student @ CUET. Prior to this, I completed my undergraduate @ RUET, mentored by Professor Dr. Kamal Hosain. Recently, I had the opportunity to work as a visiting research student @ Neural Interface Technology Lab, University of Florida, working closely with Dr. Adam Khalifa, Dr. Yuheng Bu & Dr. Erin Patrik on projects including Ai-based optimization tool development for electromagnetic coil design of low-power wireless power systems and designing an AI-aided electrode geometry optimization tool for neuromodulation applications.

My research explores the intersection of Neuroimaging, Meuromodulation, and AI, with a focus on developing Interactive AI tool for solving diagnostic, classifying, field mapping and optimization problems. I leverage few-shot learning algorithms to work on data-scarce domains, such as brain research and healthcare systems, to solve real-world challenges with an AI-based computational tool.
